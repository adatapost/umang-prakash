float style attribute
===============================================

The "float" attribute adjust the "inline" as well as "block" element either at "left" or "right" side of web document's body.


    Syntax:

          float: left or right;


Note that the "float" attribute is instantly applies to all elements followed by it so use "clear" style attribute to remove "float" effect.


    Syntax:

          clear: both or float or right;
